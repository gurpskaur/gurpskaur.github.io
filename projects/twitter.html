Twemoji Perceptions Across Major International Markets

An exploratory study to understand whether the redesigned Twitter emojis is well received by its major international markets.

July 2021

My Role: Lead UX Researcher

Research Methods: Unmoderated Interviews via usertesting.com

Team: Gurpreet Kaur, Designers, Product Manager

...

Overview

Twitter’s emoji library is currently being redesigned (Twemoji 2021). The new emoji library is shifting from flat design to an elaborate, glossy 3D-like design. The new visual approach has been designed to be inclusive, differentiated, expressive, and scalable.

In order for Twemoji 2021 to be successful, we need to know how this new visual approach resonates with global audiences.

...

Research Objectives

  • Understand whether the redesigned Twemoji library represents people in the Brazil, India, and the US

  • Understand customer perspectives about the visual style of the emojis

  • Understand whether the Twemojis fit the Twitter Brand

...

Methodology

I conducted remote, unmoderated user interviews via usertesting.

  • 35 total participants: 10 Brazil, 10 India, 35 US

  • Ages ranged from 18 - 65

  • Participants were Twitter users who were familiar with Tweets and DMs. Participants regularly add emojis when sending or reacting to online msgs

Note: Unable to show emojis tested/detailed research plan due to NDA.

...

Key Insights

  • Users are more keen to use emojis in their day to day conversations when they can identify themselves with the emoji.

  • Majority of users disliked said that the emojis were excessively detailed and too realistic.

  • Users felt that the visual design of the emojis looked outdated due to the lack of color and overly expressive emotions.

...

Recommendations

Users are less likely to engage with emojis that are not visually appealing. While the new emoji refresh manages to capture some elements of modernity, boldness, and friendliness — I think it’s important to focus on creating emojis that are consistent, simple, and representative to increase engagement.

  • Be representative - Users are looking to express emotions or convey feelings with emojis they recognize and relate to.

  • Be consistent - Consistency allows users to pick and choose emojis that will work together harmoniously.

  • Be simple - When focusing on simplicity, emojis become easier to understand and easier to use, which increases engagement.

Impact

  • My recommendations and insights informed design changes

  • Asked to deliver research insights to India Research team

  • Research report was featured in the monthly Twitter Research newsletter
