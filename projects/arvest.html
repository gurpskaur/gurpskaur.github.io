Executive Summary

Arvest Bank is a bank that offers various services such as home loans, credit cards, and wealth management. It is one of the largest and oldest banks headquartered in Bentonville, Arkansas, with branches in Arkansas, Kansas, Oklahoma, and Missouri.

Poor page wayfinding can obstruct users from finding relevant products and/or deter users from visiting. As a result, the aim of the project was to evaluate the structure and organization of existing webpages and provide solutions and a low-fidelity proposal on how to improve site navigation.
