Meet Gurpreet Kaur

I’m a UX Researcher getting my masters in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at DePaul University. I received my bachelors in Neuroscience at University of California, Riverside and worked as a Chemist at ThermoFisher. Almost a year into the job I was looking for new opportunities where I could grow because I felt stuck where I was and I stumbled upon UX (I didn’t even know the field of UX existed). After doing some research, I enrolled in classes to be qualified to get into a HCI Masters program so I could pursue a career as a UX Researcher. Since my time at DePaul University, I’ve had the pleasure of working on a project with University of Chicago’s Center for Decision Research to refine the visitor experience for their new discovery center in downtown Chicago.
