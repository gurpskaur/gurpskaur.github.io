Meet Gurpreet

I’m an aspiring UX Researcher with an interest in making technology enjoyable, intuitive, and accessible for everyone. I believe that creating meaningful user experiences requires a deep understanding of consumer needs, which in turn requires a user-focused mentality. This conviction led me to pursue a Human-Computer Interaction Master’s program at DePaul University. At DePaul, I employed ethical research practices with methods such as usability testing, survey design, and interviews to explore the behaviors and motivations of users. These skills paired with my passion lead me to inspire change and bring innovation to everyday products. Let’s create change together! You can reach me on LinkedIn or via email.
