Meet Gurpreet

I’m an aspiring UX Researcher who has always had an interest in human interactions. I want to help bring focus on the user, to understand and empathize around user needs, and create meaningful user experiences. These aspirations led me to pursue a Master’s program at DePaul University majoring in Human-Computer Interaction. At DePaul I learned to conduct ethical research using methods such as usability testing, survey design, and interviews, to explore the behaviors and motivations of users. My goal is to inspire change and bring innovation to everyday products. Let’s help create change together. You can reach me at LinkedIn or email.
